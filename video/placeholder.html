Wedding Video Background Resources

To complete your wedding website, you'll need to add a video background to the hero section. Here are some resources to help you find the perfect video for your site.

Free Video Resources

  • Pexels Wedding Videos - High-quality free stock videos
  • Pixabay Wedding Videos - Free videos for commercial use
  • Videvo Wedding Videos - Free stock footage
  • Mixkit Wedding Videos - Free videos for websites

Premium Video Resources

  • Shutterstock Wedding Videos - Premium stock footage
  • iStock Wedding Videos - Premium videos with subscription options
  • Storyblocks Wedding Videos - Unlimited downloads with subscription
  • Pond5 Wedding Videos - Marketplace for video content

Tips for Selecting the Perfect Wedding Video Background:

  • Keep it subtle: Choose a video with gentle movement that won't distract from your content.
  • Consider file size: Optimize your video for web to ensure fast loading times.
  • Match your theme: Select a video that complements your wedding color scheme and style.
  • Check licensing: Ensure you have the proper rights to use the video on your website.
  • Test on mobile: Make sure your video looks good on all devices.

Once you've found your perfect video, rename it to "wedding-background.mp4" and place it in this folder. The website will automatically use it as the background for your hero section.
