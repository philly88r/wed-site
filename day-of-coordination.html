Day-of Wedding Coordination

Let us handle the details on your big day while you focus on creating memories.

How We'll Support You

Our experienced coordinators will ensure your wedding day runs smoothly, managing all the details so you can be fully present and enjoy every moment of your special day.

Professional Coordination

Our experienced coordinators will manage your wedding day from start to finish, ensuring everything runs according to plan and handling any unexpected issues that arise.

Vendor Management

We'll coordinate with all your vendors before and during the event, confirming arrival times, managing setup, and ensuring everyone knows their responsibilities.

Timeline Management

We'll create a detailed schedule for your wedding day and ensure everything happens on time, from the ceremony to the last dance.

Setup & Breakdown

We'll oversee the setup of your ceremony and reception spaces, and ensure everything is properly packed up at the end of the night.

Emergency Kit

We come prepared with a comprehensive emergency kit to handle any last-minute issues, from wardrobe malfunctions to headaches.

Peace of Mind

With our team handling the logistics, you and your loved ones can relax and fully enjoy your wedding day without stress or distractions.

Our Coordination Process

1

Initial Consultation

We'll meet to discuss your vision, needs, and expectations for your wedding day. This helps us understand how we can best support you.

2

Vendor Communication

We'll reach out to all your vendors to introduce ourselves, confirm details, and establish a point of contact for the wedding day.

3

Timeline Creation

We'll develop a detailed timeline for your wedding day, ensuring everything flows smoothly from start to finish.

4

Final Walkthrough

About 1-2 weeks before your wedding, we'll conduct a final walkthrough at your venue to finalize all details and logistics.

5

Rehearsal Coordination

We'll direct your wedding rehearsal, ensuring everyone knows their roles and responsibilities for the big day.

6

Wedding Day Management

On your wedding day, we'll be there from start to finish, managing all details and handling any issues that arise so you can focus on enjoying your day.
